Choosing the right VR headset and equipment

  • These are VR headsets that dont require any connection to a Personal computer to use with it therefore are called stand-alone headsets. Despite this, they can still be connected to a PC to play PC VR games and experiences These vr headsets need to be charged just like smartphones as they run off their own software and components.

    Advantages

    ease of use - light weight, easy to set up

    portability

    compatibility - most can be used with PC’s

    user friendly interface

    affordability - compared to other vr systems like PC VR headsets, these headsets are priced at a much reasonable price.

    wireless

    additional accessories can be bought to improve comfort and fit

    Disadvantages

    battery life - as they are battery powered stand-alone headsets, they require to be charged

    processing power- they have lower processing power compared to High end PC VR headsets, which means lower quality and performance.

    limited content compared to PC VR headsets as they have the capability to play PC VR games
