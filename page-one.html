Welcome to the world of Virtual Reality.

What is VR?

A virtual reality device is made to be worn over your eyes and allows you to see and interact with an artificially simulated world through its lenses, making you feel as if you have stepped into a different world beyond imagination. The device comes in the form of a specially designed type of headset or goggles with computer hardware and software components fit into them to create a computer-generated and intractable environment (Sheldon, 2022). The device can be used to play virtual reality games, take part in virtual tours of places, use it for training in medical fields, etc.
