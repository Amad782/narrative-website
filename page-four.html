Resources

Vr application recommendations

YouTube VR- With YouTube VR there are endless opportunities, you can watch virtual tours, VR videos and any normal videos. You can either download YouTube VR from the meta store or search for VR videos in your headset's browser.


DEOVR- similar to YouTube, this is a platform where VR videos are solely published on for viewers.

Recommended Games

Walkabout mini golf- you can play mini golf with your friends and family all from the comfort of your home. The physics of the game are realistic also the controls are very user-friendly and there are many golf courses to challenge yourself. This game falls into the physical and cognitive benefits.


Beat Saber- this game revolves around slashing cubes that come towards you in the beat of a song. This game falls into the physical and cognitive benefits as it's a game that requires you to move your arms.

Real fishing VR: provides a much more realistic experience with lifelike graphics and offers a great user-friendly fishing experience. This game falls into all benefits.

Social games & apps

Big screen beta- this is a great application to watch movies, YouTube or any streaming services with others in many different settings such as cinemas, theatres and more. There are social chatrooms to join and meet people, or you can set one up yourself and invite others.
