Why now?

Now is the perfect time to get into the Virtual Reality scene. Thanks to the fast advancement of VR technology, it has become more accessible and user friendly for seniors than ever. Roll back the years again and experience places you have never been to through jaw dropping virtual tours and immersive games. Escape reality and experience things beyond your imagination, all from the comfort of your home.

A study conducted by the Sheridan Centre for Elder Research in collaboration with a national provider of retirement and LTC (long-term-care) homes, aimed to study the potential impact of VR experiences on the well being of its residents. Results of this study highlighted that these VR experiences had a positive impact on well-being in LTC homes whilst also being cost effective (Chaze et al., 2022).
